# Golden Sample Director Notes

## Purpose

This is Narrascape's fixed quality benchmark. It is intentionally small: one room,
one principal character, one emotional arc, and six shots. The goal is to measure
whether the pipeline can prepare, bind, generate, select, assemble, QA, and rework
AI-film material without hiding behind scale.

## Character Bible

### raskolnikov

- Role: former student under moral collapse.
- Age: early 20s.
- Face and body: gaunt Slavic face, hollow sleepless gray eyes, high cheekbones, pale uneven skin, damp dark-brown hair falling over the forehead, thin body, tense shoulders, nervous hands.
- Wardrobe lock: worn dark wool student coat, faded brown waistcoat, loose off-white collar, scuffed black boots, old dark cap; clothing stays poor and rumpled.
- Behavior: avoids direct eye contact, freezes suddenly, moves as if listening to an inner argument.
- Negative anchors: not handsome fashion model, not clean, not muscular, not modern, no leather trench coat, no detective styling, no visible weapon.

### sonya_memory

- Role: moral pressure remembered by Raskolnikov.
- Age: late teens to early 20s.
- Face and body: pale tired face, steady eyes, small still presence.
- Wardrobe lock: plain faded brown dress, modest gray-brown shawl, small cross at neck.
- Behavior: appears only as quiet memory or reflected moral demand.
- Negative anchors: no saintly glow, no fantasy halo, no glamour makeup, no luxury dress.

## Scene Bible

### rented_room

- Core look: Raskolnikov's cramped attic-like rented room, yellow wallpaper, sloped ceiling, narrow bed, old books, cracked cup, tiny table, threadbare coat hook, door too close to the bed.
- Lighting: sickly candle and dawn window light, yellow-green shadows, heavy chiaroscuro.
- Continuity: never spacious, never clean, never theatrical; it must feel like a pressure chamber.

## Storyboard Intent

- `sb_01_01`: Wide room pressure; Raskolnikov small in lower third, ceiling and walls bearing down.
- `sb_02_01`: Medium shot; his hands tighten the worn coat, then tremble.
- `sb_03_01`: Detail still life; candle, cracked cup, old books, and a nervous hand at frame edge.
- `sb_04_01`: Door threshold; Raskolnikov frozen between bed and door, unable to move forward.
- `sb_05_01`: Close-up memory; Sonya's steady face or small cross appears as moral pressure, not fantasy glow.
- `sb_06_01`: Dawn close-up; Raskolnikov lowers his head, the same face and coat, responsibility replacing performance.

## QA Must Check

- Raskolnikov keeps the same gaunt face, damp dark-brown hair, worn student coat, faded waistcoat, loose collar, and old cap.
- The rented room remains cramped, yellow, period-specific, and candlelit or dawn-lit.
- No modern props, cars, neon, platform labels, readable text, watermarks, or poster typography.
- No visible weapon appears in the sample.
- Every generated clip must map to a storyboard frame and a director contract shot.
